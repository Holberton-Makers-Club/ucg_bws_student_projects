Dreamland Theater

The only colored theatre in the city.

About Us

We’re located at 127 N. Greenwood. We have a seating capacity of 750, with movie tickets costing 15 cents! We hold not only silent films but also live performances. Our theatre has also served as a community gathering spot.

Owner Bio

Loula Williams and her husband John Wesley were some of Greenwood’s first residents. John was a mechanic and Loula was a teacher. Soon Loula decided to make a career change, she opened a confectionary.
