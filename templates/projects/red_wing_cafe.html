Owner Bio

The proprietors of the Red Wing Café were listed as Tillman & Lewis in some ads, while Julius T. Presley was listed in other Red Wing Café ads as the owner. Neither had any background information except it was reported in the Tulsa Star Newspaper that in April of 1920 J.T. Presley “purchased a swell electric piano for the Red Wing Café.” In this same newspaper it was stated that J.T. Presley was a joint proprietor owner of the Red Wing Hotel and Café along with his mother Mrs. Quinty Presley. She was described as ”one of the most progressive’ women of Tulsa and has a large circle of friends here.”
