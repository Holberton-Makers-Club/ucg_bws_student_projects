About Us

Stop by 121 N. Greenwood Ave and we'll make sure your shoes come out shiny and new. While you’re here, check out our extensive shoe collection and our very popular collection of Black Swan Records sold in the shop. Owners James and Vasinora Nails Sr, along with Henry Nails will make sure you leave a very happy customer.

Owner Bio

The Nails owned several businesses in Greenwood including the Nails Dance Pavilion and Skating Rink, as well as a Limousine and Taxi Service. James H. Nails was a proud college educated shoemaker, who was said to be able to take a piece of leather and make a shoe that anyone would be proud of. The owners did rebuild after the massacre, and a plaque with the business’ name can be found in Greenwood.
