Owner Bio

M.L. Cain personally roomed at the Red Wing Hotel. Cain’s Café was located in a small one-story frame building owned by a prominent woman of color, Mrs. Partee. At the time of the massacre, the building contained two spaces for two businesses- Cain's Café and a Dr. R.T. Motley’s Office.

Cain’s Café is only recorded in Tulsa’s 1921 directory, although the space had previously been Ideal Café with a different proprietor the year before.

Mrs. Partee claimed $1,500 in losses of that building resulting from the Massacre (about $21,820 in losses today, 2020). Mrs. Partee was one of the first to rebuild, however, no documentation was found indicating M.L. Cain stayed in Tulsa after the massacre or ever rebuilt Cain’s Café.
